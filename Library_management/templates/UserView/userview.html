History of Sharjah Library

The Sharjah Public Library was established in 1989 as part of Sharjah's cultural development efforts. It began as a small community library and has since grown into a significant cultural and knowledge center. The library is part of the Sharjah Libraries Network, which includes several specialized libraries across the emirate. It offers a wide range of services, including digital resources, events, and programs for all age groups. The library reflects Sharjah's commitment to fostering education, innovation, and cultural exchange.
